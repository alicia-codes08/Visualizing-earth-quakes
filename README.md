I visualized earth quakes from the last hour in eq_past_hour.py and in eq_past_week.py the earth quake data from the past week.
The goal of this project was to show the earth quakes on this planet clear through color and size. 
Through the different sizes and colors of these dots one can assume that bigger and brighter dots mean stronger earth quakes 
while smaller and darker ones are rather weak. Through these two traits and the placement of these dots one can assume that 
earth quakes of this kind occur more often on these places than on others. 

Data is from the following link: https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/geojson.php
